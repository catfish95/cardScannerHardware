## Assembly Notes

### Material Specifications
- **Primary Material:** PLA/PETG (for 3D printed parts)
- **Hardware:** Steel fasteners and shafts
- **Bearings & Motion:** Ball bearings, O-rings, flex couplings

### Plate Layout
- **Plate 1:** Core structural elements (shafts, rollers, gears)
- **Plate 2:** Enclosure and panel components
- **Plate 3:** Magazine and storage systems
- **Plate 4:** External covers, support structures, retention and clamping mechanisms
- **Optional:** Smartphone integration and lighting accessories

### Printing Recommendations
- Use high-quality PLA/PETG filament for durability
- Print at appropriate layer heights for mechanical fit tolerance
- Consider printing support structure parts with reinforcement
